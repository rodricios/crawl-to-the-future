 Keepsake Pages
Give any front page from the past 150 years! Celebrate a birthday, anniversary, or any other milestone with a New York Times front page reproduction from any date since 1851.
Keepsake Pages, reduced from the size of the original paper for convenient presentation, are printed on high-quality 11 x 17 inch photographic paper.
Pages dating from 1998 to the present are in color, like the paper itself.
Framed pages, measuring 13 x 19 inches, include a white mat, a black wood frame and glass glazing.
Full-scale pages are also available, printed on 18 x 24 inch photographic paper (not available framed).
