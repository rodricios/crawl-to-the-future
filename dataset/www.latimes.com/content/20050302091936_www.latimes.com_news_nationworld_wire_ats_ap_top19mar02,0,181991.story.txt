Ginobili, Spurs Outgun Raptors 92-86

	Manu Ginobili scored 17 points, and Tony Parker added 15 to lead the San Antonio Spurs to a 92-86 victory over the Toronto Raptors on Wednesday night. Tim Duncan and Robert Horry each added 13 points to help the NBA-leading Spurs improve to 44-13 overall and 25-2 at the SBC Center. Chris Bosh led Toronto with 16 points. The Raptors, who shot 24 percent from the floor in the first half, had won their previous three games. The Spurs led by as many as 21 points in the third quarter. In the first meeting of the season between the teams, Toronto overcame a 20-point deficit to beat the visiting Spurs 96-91 on Nov. 21. Notes:@ The Spurs opened a four-game homestand, their longest stretch of games at the SBC Center so far this season. ... Spurs forward Nazr Mohammed, acquired from New York last week, had two points and one rebound in 6 minutes.
