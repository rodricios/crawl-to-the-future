Reuters Photo - Wednesday 18 October 2000
Galleries: Top Stories | World | Sports | Entertainment | Public Opinion

Filipino protesters opposed to Philippine President Joseph Estrada call for his resignation during a rally of about 10,000 people in Manila's Makati financial district, October 18, 2000. Philippine opposition lawmakers started impeachment proceedings against Estrada on October 18, listing various charges including that he took millions of dollars in bribes from gambling lords. (Erik De Castro/Reuters)
